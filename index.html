Poor Mans Pizza Recipe

Ingredients Directions Mailing List

A cheap, quick and easy recipe for a pizza style snack.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomato Puree
  • Cheese
  • Any other toppings of your choice

Directions

  1. Toast the bread on both sides
  2. While the bread is toasting pre heat grill to high
  3. Grate the cheese
  4. Once the bread is toasted spread a layer of tomato puree on one side
  5. Sprinkle grated cheese ontop (as much or as little as you like)
  6. Place any other toppings you want on top
  7. Plcae under grill for 3-5 min or until the cheese has melted
  8. Enjoy!
